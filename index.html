ABOUT US

InnovateHer, hosted by seven student organizations and the Purdue student government, is a 36-hour hackathon with a mission to create a welcoming and inclusive space for students of marginalized identities in technology. We are dedicated to encouraging everyone to recognize and create solutions for issues faced by Women, as well as celebrating their remarkable contributions to technology.

FAQs

How do I apply?
Applications will open on December 1, 2023 and close on February 2, 2024.
What should I bring?
This Will Be An Overnight Event So We Suggest Bringing A Blanket, Device Chargers, Purdue ID, And Of Course Your Laptop!
How large can my
team be?
You Can Have A Maximum Of 5 People On Your Team.
Do I need to be a CS
major to apply?
You Do Not Need To Be A CS Major To Apply. All Majors Are Welcome To Apply!
how does the judging
process work?
We Will Have Certain Criteria That Will Be Used To Evaluate The Projects. There Will Be A Panel Of Judges (Sponsors, Experts, Organizers, Etc.) Who Will Score Each Project And Evaluate It Based On The Criteria.)
Who should I ask
when I am stuck?
We Will Have Mentors During The Hackathon For Any Help You Need. If You Have Any Other Questions, Feel Free To DM Us On Our Instagram At @Innovatherhacks Or Email Us At Innovateherhacks@Gmail.Com
Where and when will
InnovateHer take place?
The Hackathon Will Take Place At Stewart Center On March 2nd And March 3rd! We Will Also Be Using The Memorial Mall And Meredith South Multipurpose Room Post 11:30 PM. Keep An Eye Out For The Schedule!
What is a women
centric hackathon?
It’s An Event Whose Main Objective Is To Encourage And Empower Women In The Field Of Technology And Innovation. It Will Provide A Supportive And Inclusive Environment To Promote Gender Diversity And Create A Platform Where Women Can Showcase Their Skills
How much experience
do I need to have?
Our Event Is Completely Beginner Friendly! We Will Be Having Multiple Coding Worskhops Too, So Even If You Have 0 Experience, That’s Alright :))
who can Participate?
EVERYONE Can Participate! InnovateHer Is A Women-Centric Hackathon, But All Individuals Are Welcome, Regardless Of How They Identify. Our Primary Aim Is To Establish A Safe And Inclusive Space For Minorities In STEM. To Ensure That The Presence Of The Non-Target Demographic Doesn’t Overshadow This Goal, We Will Implement A Code Of Conduct. Additionally, It Will Be Mandatory For All Participants To Attend At Least One Diversity, Equity, And Inclusion (DEI) Talk To Gain Insights Into Current Issues And Potential Solutions.

We Aim To Spotlight The Challenges Faced By Women And Non-Binary People In Technology. It’s Evident That Contemporary Tech Landscapes Harbor Gender-Related Challenges, Especially Regarding Bias And Underrepresentation. Through This Event, We Hope To Embolden All Students And Promote Active Participation Towards Closing The Gender Gap In STEM.
